I love the creative process, and I use that passion to build designs with functionality and personality.

I love to design for web, print, and motion, and it delights me when my work impacts others positively. When I’m not busy designing or chasing my toddler around, you’ll find me listening to podcasts, playing the ukulele, or attempting to knit.
