I love the creative process, and I use that passion to build designs with functionality and personality.

I grew up thinking I would become a full time painter or illustrator… and then I fell in love with graphic design. I love to design for both print and web, and it delights me when my work impacts others positively. When I’m not designing or entertaining my daughter, you’ll find me listening to podcasts, playing the ukulele, or practising hand-drawn typography.
